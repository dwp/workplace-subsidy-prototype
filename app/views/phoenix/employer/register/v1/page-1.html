Search for an occupational health provider

Use this service to find a provider who can give you a discounted occupational health assessment for your employee. 

Select a provider from the list, then use the details provided to contact them directly. The provider will talk to you about:

  • how complex your employee's situation is
  • who they recommend to do the assessment - this could be a doctor, nurse or other health professional
  • whether they need to see your employee in person - this could be to assess their workspace

Based on your discussion, the provider will give you a quote for the cost of the assessment. This will be between £100 and £400. After discount, the amount you pay will be between £20 and £80.

The provider will invoice you. When paying, give the provider your 80% discount code and pay them the other 20%. The provider will claim the rest from the government. 

If your employee does not attend the appointment as arranged, you will have to pay 100% of the cost.
