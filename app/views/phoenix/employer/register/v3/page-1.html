Find an occupational health provider

Search the list to find a provider who can give you a discounted occupational health assessment for your employee.  

Select a provider then use the details provided to contact them directly. The provider will talk to you about:

  • how complex your employee's situation is
  • who they recommend to do the assessment - this could be a doctor, nurse or other health professional

They'll also talk to you about whether they need to see your employee in person. This could be for a range of reasons, such as: 

  • your employee has communication difficulties
  • the provider may need to assess their workspace

What the assessment will cost

If you’re eligible for the 80% discount, the assessment will usually cost between £20 and £80.  

The actual cost of an occupational health (OH) assessment from our service is normally between £100 and £400.

Why the cost of an assessment varies
The cost of an assessment may vary due to factors such as:
  • the method of delivery. Most assessments will be by phone or video conference. However, some assessments may need an in-person or onsite meeting. These will usually involve an extra cost.
  • the complexity of the case. For example, a case involving a broken arm may be more straightforward than a case involving a sensitive mental health condition.
  • who carries out the assessment. Some assessments can be done by a physiotherapist, while others may require a nurse or a doctor.

The final cost will usually be discussed and agreed upon during the initial conversation between the employer and the OH provider they have selected.

The 80% discount only applies up to an assessment cost of £400.

In some cases where an in-person assessment has been agreed, there may be additional travel costs. Any further costs over £400 are not discounted.

See examples of how the discount works

Example 1

A telephone assessment by a physiotherapist.
Cost £100. Final cost to employer with 80% discount = £20

Example 2

A video conference assessment by a doctor.
Cost £300. Final cost to employer with 80% discount = £60

Example 3

An in-person assessment by a doctor, at the employee’s workplace.
Cost £350 plus £100 travel costs. Final cost to employer with 80% discount = £130

In this case the total cost including travel is £450. The 80% discount applies up to £400, and the remaining £50 cost above this is paid in full by the employer.

If your employee does not attend the appointment as arranged, you will have to pay 100% of the cost. However, regular and open engagement with your employee about the assessment process can reduce the chances of non-attendance.
