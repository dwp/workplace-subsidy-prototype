Payment process     

If you are accepted for the scheme, you will be sent comprehensive information on the payment and invoicing process.

In general, it will work like this:

  • once an assessment has been agreed your normal invoicing schedule should apply. 
  • you need to invoice the client for: 
    • - 20% of the cost of the assessment, including any travel costs, up to the £400 limit   
    • - 100% of any travel costs over the £400 limit 
  • to claim the rest of the cost: 
    • - email an invoice, with the discount code provided by the client included on it, to Shared Services Connected Ltd (SSCL), copying in DWP 

After invoicing you should expect payment within 10 working days.
