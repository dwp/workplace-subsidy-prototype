If you are accepted to join the scheme     

We will notify you and ask you for information about your organisation so you can be listed in the service and set up as a supplier on our payment system.   

This information will include details such as: 

  • organisation name 
  • contact details 
  • web address 
  • office and clinic locations 
  • bank details 
  • assessment price range 
  • type of assessments offered 
  • cancellation policy
